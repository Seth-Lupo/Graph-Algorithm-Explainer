The Traveling Salesperson Problem

An Interactive Simulation

By Seth Lupo

*This is a modified version of the TSP problem. Here, the sale's person's route can revisit nodes and edges. Also, the sales person does not need to visit every node, only specified nodes (houses). More formally, we are finding the closed walk that contains all specified vertices of least cost, not the Hamiltonian cycle of least cost. Though this is more difficult to solve, as it requires a specific preemptive steps, I find it to be more interesting and useful. I will explain the differences in full in the article.*
